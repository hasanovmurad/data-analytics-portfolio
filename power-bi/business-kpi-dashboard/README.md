# Business KPI Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents a Business KPI Dashboard developed in Power BI using sales data. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance across regions, product categories, and time periods.

## KPIs

* Total Sales
* Total Quantity Sold
* Average Discount

## Visualizations

* Sales by Region
* Sales by Product Category
* Sales Trend Over Time

## Key Insights

* North region generated the highest sales volume.
* South region generated the lowest sales volume.
* Clothing was the best-performing product category.
* Food generated the lowest sales volume among product categories.
* The average discount was 13.72%.

## Tools Used

* Power BI Desktop
* CSV Dataset
